Scheduling a consultation with Investra is a straightforward process designed to accommodate your needs. Clients can reach out through our website’s contact form, where they can provide details about their legal issue and preferred contact method. Consultations are typically available in-person, over the phone, or via video conferencing, ensuring accessibility and convenience.
