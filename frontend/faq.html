FAQs

How do I sign up for Investra?

Click the "Sign Up" button on the homepage, enter your email and password, and follow the prompts to create a free account.

Are Investra courses free?

Yes, all courses are free and accessible after signing in. They cover topics like stock analysis, dividends, and market myths.

What is the Virtual Stocks feature?

Virtual Stocks lets you simulate trading with fake money. You can build a portfolio and practice investing without financial risk.

Do I need prior knowledge to use Investra?

No, Investra is designed for beginners. Our courses and glossary provide clear explanations to help you start learning.

How can I report a bug or issue?

Visit the Help Center and use the Feedback & Suggestions form to report bugs or contact support for assistance.

What are stocks?

Stocks are shares of ownership in a company. Buying stocks allows you to benefit from the company’s growth and profits.

How often are new courses added?

New courses are added periodically. Check the Announcements section in the Help Center for updates on new content.

Can I interact with other users on Investra?

Yes, the Community Forum in the Help Center allows you to ask questions, share tips, and discuss investing with others.
